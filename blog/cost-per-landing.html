Aircraft Tyre Cost Per Landing – Complete Guide

By AeroTyre Technical Team | Chennai

What is Cost Per Landing?

Cost per landing is the total tyre purchase cost divided by the number of safe landings the tyre completes.

Example Calculation

If a main wheel tyre costs ₹1,50,000 and lasts 250 landings:

₹1,50,000 ÷ 250 = ₹600 per landing

What Affects Tyre Life?

  • Runway surface condition
  • Landing technique
  • Aircraft weight
  • Tyre pressure management
  • Braking intensity

How Operators Reduce Cost

  • Proper inflation checks
  • Regular inspection
  • Retreading programs
  • Correct storage practices
