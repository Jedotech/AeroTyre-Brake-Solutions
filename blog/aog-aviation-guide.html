What Is AOG in Aviation?

Aircraft On Ground Emergency Response Explained

AOG (Aircraft on Ground) is one of the most critical situations in aviation operations. It occurs when an aircraft becomes grounded due to a technical issue requiring immediate repair.

Common Causes of AOG

  • Tyre failures
  • Brake system defects
  • Hydraulic component issues
  • Avionics malfunctions

Operational Impact

When an aircraft is AOG, airlines experience significant operational losses. Each hour of delay can cost thousands of dollars.

Why Rapid Parts Supply Matters

  • Minimize aircraft downtime
  • Protect flight schedules
  • Reduce passenger disruption
